How to Make a Sandwich ?!
  1. Gather your ingredients:
    • Bread
    • Meat
    • Cheese
    • Lettuce
    • Tomato
    • Condiments
  2. Place two slices of bread on a clean cutting board.
    • Tip: Use a cutting board to avoid damaging your countertop. Bread
  3. Add your desired amount of meat on one slice of bread.
    • Tip: Choose deli meats like turkey, ham, or roast beef. Meat
  4. Place a slice of cheese on top of the meat.
    • Tip: Try different types of cheese like cheddar, Swiss, or provolone. Cheese
  5. Add lettuce and tomato slices on top of the cheese.
    • Tip: Wash the lettuce and tomato before adding them to your sandwich. Lettuce , Tomato
  6. Spread your favorite condiments (like mayonnaise or mustard) on the other slice of bread.
    • Tip: Experiment with different condiments for unique flavors. Condiments
  7. Carefully place the second slice of bread on top of the sandwich, condiment side down.
    • Tip: Align the edges of the bread for a neat presentation.
